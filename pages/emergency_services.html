Emergency Preparation Checklist

What to do while waiting for your emergency technician

Safety First

  • Turn off power/gas if safe to do so
  • Shut off water if there's flooding
  • Ventilate area if gas smell detected
  • Keep area clear for technician access

Document the Issue

  • Take photos of error codes/damage
  • Note when the problem started
  • Record any unusual sounds/smells
  • Gather appliance model/serial numbers

Prepare for Technician

  • Clear path to appliance location
  • Locate warranty/service manuals
  • Ensure good lighting in work area
  • Have payment method ready
